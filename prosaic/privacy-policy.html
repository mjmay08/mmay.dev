Privacy Policy for Prosaic

Effective Date: 07/17/2025

Prosaic (“the App”) is developed and published by Mitchell May. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection

Prosaic uses Google Analytics to collect basic, non-personal usage data to help improve the experience and performance of the App. This may include:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Feature usage and app screen views
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • General location (e.g., country or region)

No personal information, such as your name, email, or precise location, is collected.

2. Data Usage

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Understand how users interact with the App
  • Identify bugs and improve performance
  • Make informed design and feature decisions

3. Data Sharing

Collected data is processed by Google Analytics, subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. Prosaic does not share your data with other third parties, advertisers, or marketers.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All analytics data is stored securely by Google Analytics. Users do not create accounts and cannot directly access, modify, or delete analytics data. No user-specific or personally identifiable data is stored on your device or externally.

5. Children

Prosaic is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that data has been collected from a child, please contact us and we will investigate and take appropriate action.

6. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or app store listing.
