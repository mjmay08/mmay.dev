Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Cribbage Squares ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • No account data: Cribbage Squares does not require account creation, email addresses, usernames, or passwords.
  • Optional shared content: If you choose to use the share feature, the app can generate a screenshot of your game board and share text so you can send it through apps you select.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game statistics (local): Games played, wins, score history, streaks, best scores, and related gameplay stats are stored on your device.
  • Daily challenge and achievements (local): Daily challenge progress, streaks, and achievement unlock status are stored on your device.
  • App settings (local): Preferences such as sound mute, haptic feedback, selected theme, tutorial completion, review-prompt state, and ads-removed state are stored on your device.
  • Analytics data: Anonymous gameplay and interaction events are collected through Firebase Analytics (for example: game start/completion, score-related events, achievement unlocks, and settings toggles).
  • Advertising data: If ads are shown, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) may collect device and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.
  • Purchase metadata: In-app purchase status (such as whether ads were removed) is handled by Apple App Store / Google Play and a local entitlement flag is stored on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core gameplay: Save your progress, stats, streaks, and achievements.
  • Personalize your experience: Remember your app settings and preferences.
  • Improve the app: Understand usage patterns and improve game balance, UX, and stability.
  • Monetize and support development: Show ads (unless removed) and process optional in-app purchases.
  • Enable optional features: Allow review prompts and user-initiated sharing.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may process data:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Core: Supports Firebase integration and app configuration.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects aggregated event analytics such as gameplay actions and feature usage.
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • Displays interstitial ads in supported mobile builds unless you purchase ad removal.
  • May process device identifiers, approximate location, IP address, and ad interaction information as described by Google.
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad controls: You can limit ad personalization in your device settings.

3.3 In-App Purchases (Apple / Google)

  • Purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • We do not collect or store full payment card information.
  • Store providers maintain transaction records under their own policies.

3.4 In-App Review (Apple / Google)

  • The app may open the platform store listing when you choose to leave a review.
  • Any ratings, reviews, or account data submitted there are handled by the platform provider.

3.5 Share Sheet Integrations

  • If you choose to share results, the app creates share content (text and optional image) and passes it to apps/services you choose.
  • Those third-party apps process shared content under their own privacy terms.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage on Device

Most app data is stored locally on your device using platform preferences storage:

  • Game stats and score history
  • Achievements and daily challenge data
  • Settings (sound, haptics, theme, tutorial state)
  • Purchase entitlement state (for ad removal)
  • Review prompt eligibility/shown flags

4.2 Networked Data

  • Analytics and advertising traffic is sent to Firebase/Google services over secure HTTPS/TLS connections.
  • There is no user account backend or multiplayer profile database operated by us for this app.

4.3 Security Measures

  • We use reputable platform and SDK providers for analytics, ads, and billing.
  • No in-app password system is used by Cribbage Squares.
  • Data stored locally remains under your device security controls.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only as needed:

  • Service providers: Firebase Analytics, Google Mobile Ads, Apple App Store, and Google Play.
  • User-directed sharing: Apps/services you choose through your device share sheet.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights/safety.

6. Children's Privacy

Cribbage Squares is available to a broad audience and does not require account registration. We do not knowingly collect personally identifying information directly from children under 13 through an account system. Third-party SDKs (such as ads/analytics) may process device-level information as described in their policies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Local Data Control

  • Use in-app reset options (where available) to clear gameplay data.
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from your device.

7.2 Advertising and Analytics Controls

  • Purchase "Remove Ads" to disable in-app ads in Cribbage Squares.
  • Adjust ad personalization and tracking permissions in device settings:
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

8. Data Retention

  • Local app data: Retained until you clear it in app (where applicable) or uninstall.
  • Analytics data: Retained according to Firebase/Google Analytics settings and policies.
  • Ad and purchase records: Retained by Google/Apple according to their operational and legal requirements.

9. International Data Transfers

Third-party providers we use may process data on servers in multiple countries. By using the app, you understand that data may be transferred internationally under those providers' terms.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

13. Legal Basis for Processing
